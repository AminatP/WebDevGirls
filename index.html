Easy Access

Easy Access is a website targeted to students and parents who are using online learning for the school year. Many students and parents are new to this type of learning system and have never used or don't know how to use these new applications that the students have to utilize. Easy Access allows for these students and parents to obtain information on how to use applications such as Google Classroom, Google Meets, and Zoom all in an easy and acessable format.

Union City Immigration

Union City Immigration is a website aimed towards helping immigrants settle in Union City. Many immigrants that come to a new place do not have sufficient knowledge to find even their daily needs. Nevertheless they try to make their way around the city. Therefore, in order to help these immigrants adjust to their city, a website that is to be used as a guide to living in Union City,was developed for new immigrants. Union City Immigration is solely based upon providing information for immigrants on how to find their basic needs such as clothing and food in Union City.
