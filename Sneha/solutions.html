Solutions

After hearing just how big of a problem climate change is, you might feel like we may never be able to tackle a problem this big. Do not fall into despair though, there are still many solutions that help with the fight against climate change.

Renewable Resources

Energy sources such as solar, wind, hydropower and more are all examples of renewable energy sources. Renewable energy or clean energy derives from natural resources such as light form the sun, the wind and water. These resources are sustainable because they can be constantly replenshied. Meaning you can always have it; for example you can never run out of sunlight or wind. Fossil fuels on the other hand can and will run out eventually whch means those who deppend on it (otherwise known as humans) for energy will eventually not have the resources for their electricty and heating and similar things. When you use renewable resources you have a guarantee that it won't run out. All in all: SWITCH TO RENEWABLE ENERGY!

Sustainable Transportation

Sustainable transportation is green transportation. Any means of transportation that has a low impact on the environment. Some examples include: walking, cycling, transit, carpool, and energy-efficient vechiles like electric cars. As many cars and other forms of transportation contribute to pollution, it is very important to try and lower your carbon footprint. Try alternate forms of transport. It reduces the world's energy consumption and carbon dioxide emmissions, to ensure cleaner air for all. So keep in mind to SWITCH TO A CLEAN, GREEN MACHINE!

Pricing Carbon

In short, pricing carbon is like a tax on pollution. It is a cost on the amount of carbon pollution one can emit. This motivates polluters to reduce their carbon emmisions. This innovative method is one of the most efficient ways to cap global warming. It is an easy and as mentioned earlier an efficient way to lower pollution. This strategy is already being put in use and has a promising outlook. Hopefully, this tax will help put a stop to climate change. To wrap things up: BE NICE AND PAY THE CARBON PRICE!

Protecting Forests

As we know, deforestation is big contributor to global warming. Protecting these forests are important. This solution helps to preserve and protect the forest. Our tall green friends are dying. Around 1 football field of trees is lost per second. In 2016 we lost 28million hecatres of trees. We use them for paper, pencils, furniture etc. There are so many items we use trees for. The demand is overwhelming, that's why it's so important to protect these forest. Certain forests or sections of forests are being protected and you can not harm the trees there. This way we are still preserving the wonders that trees have to offer us. Always: STAY A TREE HUGGER!
