How can You Help?

Now that you understand that climate change is a big problem that impacts everyone, you might be wondering about what you can do to help. Well, there are a variety of options, whether it be little things or huge ones. Remember that no matter what you do, you are still making a big change and helping the Earth flourish.

Some Things That You Can Do

  • Speak up and speak out! Raise awareness about climate change.
  • Power your home with renewable energy
  • Reduce water waste. Turn off the tap. Take shorter showers.
  • Try to turn off all the lights and only use them when you need them. Resort to natural lighting.
  • Reduce, reuse and recycle when you can!
  • Try to carpool, and use public transportation. Reduce your gasoline usage.
