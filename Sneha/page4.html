How can You Help?

Now that you understand that climate change is a big problem that impacts everyone, you might be wondering about what you can do to help. Well, there are a variety of options, whether it be little things or huge ones. Remember that no matter what you do, you are still making a big change and helping the Earth flourish.

Some Things That You Can Do

  • 1. Speak up and speak out! Raise awareness about climate change.
  • 2. Switch to renewable energy. Fuel your home with renewable energy sources to go green!
  • 3. Reduce water waste. Turn off the tap. Take shorter showers.
  • 4. Try to turn off all the lights and only use them when you need them.
  • 5. Eat all of your food to reduce food waste. Try to make less of your diet meat.
  • 6. Pull your plugs. Save energy and electricty!
  • 7. Reduce, reuse and recycle when you can!
  • 8. Try to carpool, and use public transportation. Reduce your gasoline usage.
