What is a PORTFOLIO?

A portfolio is a meaningful collection of Student's work that symbolizes the student's interests, range of skills, attitudes and development over a period of time.

There are three main types of PORTFOLIOS.

1. Documentation Portfolio: Collection of work demonstrating student's growth and improvement over time.

2. Process Portfolio: Contains evidence to prove the learning outcomes in the given time.

3. Showcase Portfolio: It demonstrates the best of student's outputs and products.

What to include in your PORTFOLIO?

  • Resume
  • Reference List
  • Letters of references
  • Accomplishments
  • Brochures
  • Conferences, workshops, and seminars attended
  • Public presentations or performances
  • Performance reports from internships or job evaluations
  • Description of academic study
  • Description of internships
  • Featured articles
  • Honors, awards, and scholarship achievemetns
  • In-class projects
  • Licenses or certificates for specific tranings
  • Transcripts
  • Writing samples
  • Professional Affiliations
