Tips on Saving Money

Money is an essential living expense. However, many people do not know how to save money.
SAVE MONEY! DO NOT SPEND!

  • Write down your expenses. Keep track of all your expenses and diminish the costs you do not need
  • Set saving goals for each month
  • Cut down on groceries and use coupons
  • Use the small bills and save the bigger bills in the bank account
  • Place a dollar in a big jar every day, and increase the amount every week
  • Do not buy things that you do not really need
