About Us

We've created a platform targeted to students and parents who are using online learning for the school year. Many students and parents are new to this type of learning system and have never used or don't know how to use these new applications that the students have to utilize. Easy Access allows for these students and parents to obtain information on how to use applications such as Google Classroom, Google Meet, and Zoom all in an easy and acessable format.

Education

With Easy Access students get the most out of their education so they can be the best student they can be in school.

Efficient

Students and parents can get their questions about online education applications such as Google Classroom, Google Meet, and Zoom answered in the click of a button.

Easy

Easy Access provides easy to follow, step by step videos on how to troubleshoot all different kinds of problems. Videos include what you need to know on how to unitilize the necessary features on Google Classroom, Google Meet, and Zoom.
